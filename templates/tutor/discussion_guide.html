How to lead a Socratic discussion

These questions are spoken, not written — the student never fills them out. Ask, then listen. Don't settle for "I dunno" — rephrase and wait. There are no wrong opinions, only unsupported ones, so keep asking "What in the book makes you think that?" Let the talk wander where it naturally wants to go; you don't have to get through every question.
